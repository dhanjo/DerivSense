# Coaching Note

## Exact Agent Quote
"我们需要您再提交一份银行对账单"

## What the Agent Said
The agent's response indicated that they required the customer to submit a bank statement again. While the agent's tone was neutral, the request did not acknowledge the customer's previous efforts to submit the required document. This lack of recognition contributed negatively to the customer's sentiment, as reflected in the post-pivot sentiment score.

## What the Agent Should Have Said
"我理解您已经三次提交了银行对账单，并对此感到沮丧。为了帮助您解决账户冻结的问题，我们能否请您再次提交一份银行对账单吗？我们会确保这次记录到您的档案中。"

## Missed Coaching Technique
**Empathy with Resolution**: This technique involves expressing understanding of the customer’s feelings while also providing a clear path forward. It helps to validate the customer's frustration and builds rapport.

## Why the Alternative Reduces Escalation
The alternative response acknowledges the customer's frustration and reinforces that their efforts are recognized. By showing empathy and providing assurance that their documentation will be recorded properly, the message aligns with the customer's needs. This approach helps to create a collaborative atmosphere, which can significantly reduce feelings of irritation and lead to a more positive interaction overall, thereby preventing escalation.
