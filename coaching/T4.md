# Coaching Note

## Exact Agent Quote
"很抱歉，我没有看到记录"

## What the Agent Said
The agent's response indicated that they were unaware of any records related to the customer's previous submissions. The tone of this response came across as somewhat dismissive. By apologizing without providing any additional context or next steps, the agent failed to address the customer's frustration, contributing to an escalation in the customer's sentiment from -0.5 to -1.0.

## What the Agent Should Have Said
"我很理解您对重复提交文件感到沮丧。为了更好地帮助您，我将仔细检查我们的系统并确保我们能够找到您的记录。请您稍等一下，我将帮助您解决这个问题。"

## Missed Coaching Technique
**Empathy with Resolution**: This technique involves acknowledging the customer's feelings and frustrations while offering a solution to their issue. It helps to validate the customer's concerns and demonstrates a commitment to resolving their problem.

## Why the Alternative Reduces Escalation
The improved response shows empathy towards the customer's frustration by recognizing their difficulty and validating their feelings regarding the repeated submissions. By offering to check the system and find the missing records, the agent provides a clear next step, which reassures the customer that they are being heard and that their issue will be addressed. This proactive and supportive approach is likely to reduce the customer's negative sentiment by making them feel more valued and engaged in the resolution process.
