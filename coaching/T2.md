# Coaching Note

## Exact Agent Quote
"Veo que sus documentos fueron rechazados por baja resolución"

## What the Agent Said
The agent informed the customer that their documents had been rejected due to low resolution. This response carries a neutral to negative tone, as it delivers bad news without any acknowledgment of the customer's frustration or concern. The communication appears factual and lacks emotional resonance, which likely contributed to the customer's increasing frustration.

## What the Agent Should Have Said
"Entiendo lo frustrante que es esta situación. Lamentablemente, veo que sus documentos fueron rechazados por baja resolución. No se preocupen, vamos a resolverlo juntos. ¿Podría enviar los documentos nuevamente en una calidad más alta para que podamos proceder con la verificación?"

## Missed Coaching Technique
**Empathy with Resolution**: This technique involves acknowledging the customer's feelings and frustrations while simultaneously providing a clear path forward. By expressing understanding for the customer's situation, the agent can create a sense of partnership in solving the issue.

## Why the Alternative Reduces Escalation
The alternative response reduces escalation by validating the customer's feelings and showing that the agent is aware of and empathetic to their frustration. It transforms a potentially blame-laden message into one of collaboration and support. By promising a clear next step—resending higher-quality documents—the agent not only alleviates the customer's concerns but also reassures them that a solution is within reach. This shift in tone can significantly improve the user's sentiment and reduce their likelihood of escalating the issue further.
