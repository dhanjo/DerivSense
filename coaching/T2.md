# Coaching Note

## Exact Agent Quote
"Veo que sus documentos fueron rechazados por baja resolución"

## What the Agent Said
The agent informed the user that their documents were rejected due to low resolution. This response, while factual, has a somewhat neutral to negative tone as it does not express empathy or concern for the situation the customer is facing, especially considering they have been waiting for three days.

## What the Agent Should Have Said
"I understand your frustration. Unfortunately, it seems that your documents were rejected because the resolution was too low. I apologize for the inconvenience. Let’s resolve this together. Could you please resend the documents in a higher quality? This way, we can expedite the verification process."

## Missed Coaching Technique
**Empathy with Resolution:** This technique involves acknowledging the customer's feelings and expressing understanding of their situation, while also guiding them towards a solution.

## Why the Alternative Reduces Escalation
By using an empathetic tone and acknowledging the customer's frustration, the improved response helps to humanize the interaction and validates the customer's feelings. This approach would likely soften their negative sentiment, making them feel heard and understood. Additionally, by providing a clear next step—to resend the documents—coupled with an apology for the inconvenience, the agent can help alleviate the customer's distress, thus reducing the likelihood of further escalation.
