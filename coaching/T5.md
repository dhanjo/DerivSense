# Coaching Note

## Exact Agent Quote
"أهلاً، البونص يصرف خلال 48 ساعة من الإيداع"

## What the Agent Said
The agent responded with a statement indicating that the bonus is processed within 48 hours of deposit. The tone of this response could be perceived as somewhat dismissive and factual, lacking any acknowledgment of the customer's frustration or concerns about not having received the bonus after five days.

## What the Agent Should Have Said
"أفهم أنك قلق بشأن البونص، ومن المؤسف أن عليك الانتظار. عادةً ما يتم صرف البونص خلال 48 ساعة من الإيداع، لكن إذا مر أكثر من ذلك، دعني أتحقق لك من حالة الحساب لأخدمك بشكل أفضل."

## Missed Coaching Technique
**Empathy with Resolution**: This technique involves expressing understanding and concern for the customer's feelings while providing a pathway to resolution. In this case, the agent should have acknowledged the customer's concerns about the delay in receiving the bonus.

## Why the Alternative Reduces Escalation
The improved response would de-escalate the situation by validating the customer's feelings of frustration and confusion regarding the delay. By stating, "أفهم أنك قلق بشأن البونص" (I understand that you are concerned about the bonus), the agent demonstrates empathy, which helps build rapport. Additionally, offering to check the account status provides a proactive next step, making the customer feel supported and more likely to trust the agent's assistance, thus reducing the likelihood of further escalation in the conversation.
