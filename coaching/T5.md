# Coaching Note

## Exact Agent Quote
"أهلاً، البونص يصرف خلال 48 ساعة من الإيداع"

## What the Agent Said
The agent responded to the customer's inquiry about the deposit bonus by stating that the bonus is issued within 48 hours of the deposit. The tone is somewhat transactional and lacks empathy, providing information without acknowledging the customer's frustration about not receiving the bonus after five days.

## What the Agent Should Have Said
"أفهم تمامًا أنك تشعر بالإحباط لعدم استلامك للبونص حتى الآن. عادةً، يتم صرف البونص خلال 48 ساعة من الإيداع، ولكن أحتاج إلى التحقق من حالتك الخاصة. دعني أساعدك في ذلك الآن."

## Missed Coaching Technique
**Empathy with Resolution**: This technique involves acknowledging the customer's feelings and frustrations while providing a clear next step towards resolving their issue.

## Why the Alternative Reduces Escalation
The improved response would de-escalate the situation by first showing empathy for the customer's frustration, which helps to validate their feelings. By acknowledging the delay and offering to investigate further, the agent communicates a commitment to resolving the issue rather than merely stating policy. This approach fosters a sense of support and concern for the customer's experience, which can mitigate negative emotions and lead to a more constructive dialogue.
