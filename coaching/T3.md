# Coaching Note

## Exact Agent Quote
"Halo, posisi Anda ditutup karena margin level di bawah 50%"

## What the Agent Said
The agent stated that the user's position was closed due to their margin level being below 50%. The tone of this response may come across as factual but lacks empathy and fails to acknowledge the user's distress over the unexpected loss. The message is transactional and does not address the user's strong feelings of dissatisfaction or confusion, which contributes to a negative sentiment.

## What the Agent Should Have Said
"Maaf atas ketidaknyamanan yang Anda alami. Posisi Anda ditutup otomatis untuk melindungi akun Anda karena level margin di bawah 50%, namun saya ingin membantu Anda memahami ini lebih baik. Mari kita lihat opsi yang dapat Anda ambil selanjutnya."

## Missed Coaching Technique
**Empathy with Resolution**: This technique involves expressing understanding of the customer's feelings while also guiding them toward a positive outcome. It helps to validate the customer's emotions and reinforces that the agent is here to assist.

## Why the Alternative Reduces Escalation
The improved response acknowledges the user's distress and reassures them that their situation is being taken seriously. By combining empathy with a resolution-oriented approach, such as offering to help understand the circumstances or looking at next steps, the agent diminishes feelings of frustration and abandonment. This fosters a more supportive interaction, making the customer feel seen and heard, which can lower their defensiveness and willingness to escalate the situation further.
