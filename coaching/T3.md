# Coaching Note

## Exact Agent Quote
"Saya mengerti. Sistem kami otomatis menutup posisi untuk melindungi akun Anda."

## What the Agent Said
In this response, the agent attempts to acknowledge the user's concern by starting with "Saya mengerti" (I understand). However, the explanation that follows about the system's automatic position closure comes off as mechanical and fails to address the user's emotional state or dissatisfaction. The tone is neutral but lacks empathy, leading to a disconnect with the user who is already expressing frustration over a significant financial loss.

## What the Agent Should Have Said
"I’m really sorry to hear about the loss you experienced. I understand that having your position closed unexpectedly can be very upsetting. While our system automatically closes positions to prevent further losses when the margin level falls below a certain percentage, I can help explain the situation and see if there's anything we can do to assist you further."

## Missed Coaching Technique
**Empathy with Resolution**: This technique involves acknowledging the customer's feelings while providing a pathway to address their concerns. It is crucial in situations where a customer is visibly upset, as it helps them feel heard and understood.

## Why the Alternative Reduces Escalation
The improved response reduces escalation by directly addressing the user's emotions and validating their experience. By expressing understanding and remorse, the agent builds rapport and trust. Additionally, offering to explain the situation and assist further not only conveys a willingness to help but also transforms the conversation into a collaborative problem-solving scenario. This shift decreases the likelihood of further frustration, as the customer feels supported rather than dismissed.
