# Coaching Note

### 1. Exact Agent Quote
"Thank you. I'll check this for you."

### 2. What the Agent Said
In this instance, the agent's response was polite but lacked specificity and urgency. The tone is neutral, and while it acknowledges the user, it does not convey a sense of understanding or action regarding the user's pressing concern about their missing deposit. This response failed to address the severity of the user’s complaint and did not provide any reassurance or direction.

### 3. What the Agent Should Have Said
"Thank you for providing the transaction ID, and I apologize for the inconvenience caused by your missing deposit. I will investigate this issue right away and keep you updated on the progress. I understand how important it is to resolve this quickly, especially since you need to trade. Please hold on for just a moment as I look into this."

### 4. Missed Coaching Technique
**Empathy with Resolution**: This technique involves acknowledging the customer's feelings and expressing understanding while also providing a clear path to resolution. By combining empathy with information about the next steps, agents can build trust and demonstrate commitment to solving the user's issue.

### 5. Why the Alternative Reduces Escalation
The improved response would lower user sentiment by validating their frustration and recognizing the urgency of the situation. By including an apology and committing to looking into the matter immediately, the agent shows they care about the customer’s experience and are taking their concerns seriously. This proactive approach helps to establish a connection, reduces feelings of dissatisfaction, and encourages the user to remain engaged rather than escalate the issue further.
